Who We Are

Kanata Mavericks is a new and free running club for kids in Grades 3-6.

We have created an inclusive, accessible, and fun environment in which kids can be exposed to the sport of running and be inspired to maintain a healthy active lifestyle.

Our focus is exposing kids to a variety of running workouts and fitness activities to increase their strength and endurance - all while having fun and building community. Complimentary bagels and chocolate milk will be provided at the end of each practice. Participation in community races and/or competition is completely voluntary.

Practices take place at Somerton Park, in Kanata. Runners can come on out on the dates they can and will be supervised for the duration of the practice. There is no minimum attendance requirement. Runners are responsible for their own transportation to/from practice.

Come join us for smiles and miles!
