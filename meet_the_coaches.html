Meet the Coaches

Vanessa is an elementary teacher at South March Public School. She has competed at the varsity level for Queen’s University and recently completed the Boston Marathon. As a co-founder of the Kanata Mavericks she hopes to use running as a catalyst for a generation of kids to develop resilience, build community, and challenge themselves both on and off the track. When Vanessa isn’t running herself, she can be found enjoying an espresso, playing the piano, reading a new book, or getting groans from her terribly delivery of puns.



Melanie McGee is an elementary teacher at South March Public. Her career background working with students with Autism has allowed her to develop and incomparable skill set. Her ability to connect and understand what kids need, make her a valuable coach and support for the Kanata Mavericks.
